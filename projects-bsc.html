  • Beachside Campfire

    Developed with: G27

    Released: 2023

    Experience it with the Liminal VR App on MetaQuest!

    A unique VR experience designed to assist a user in getting ready for sleep.

    Relax and doze off as you "watch" the comforting lightshow of a beach-side campfire in front of you, listening to a carefully constructed sleep-inducing soundscape.

  • My Responsibilities

    Narrative Designer

    I was responsible for designing and writing the narrative that would be spoken by the voice actors to help induce a feeling that focussed on encouraging sleep.

    Audio Engineer

    I was also responsible for recording, mixing and implementing various audio tracks that combined together to create an audioscape which enhanced the realism of the experience, as well as contributed towards helping the user get ready for sleep.

  • My Contributions

    Calming Sounds

    This project was all about getting the user ready for sleep. The audio had to be created in a way that allowed the user to feel comfortable and relaxed but also prevented them from focusing too much on the noise rather than trying to sleep, which meant recording and mixing sounds that both had to be quiet and non-intrusive, such as ocean noises without the crashing of waves, fire sounds without the peaking and crackling of the fire, subtle narration and chatting that allowed the user to feel comforted without feeling distracted, etc.
