  • The Very Hungry Anterpillar

    Developed with: L Stands for Winner

    Made for the GMTK Game Jam 2024

    Play it here!

    A 2.5D Game at a small scale!

    Crawl and Weave your way through numerous obstacles and collect as much bread as you can to avoid running out of energy, and avoid getting squashed by the boot!

  • My Responsibilities

    3D Artist

    In this Game Jam, I was responsible for the creation and texturing of the 3D models such as the Ant and other obstacles that weren't outsourced from elsewhere.

  • My Contributions

    Everyday objects

    The theme of this Game Jam was something along the lines of "drawn to scale", and so we decided to make a game from the perspective of an ant, hence making the scale of the in-game environment very small. Naturally this meant designing and modelling the 3D objects to match how they might appear to something or someone of that size, with tall and imposing profiles, exaggerated detail, etc.
