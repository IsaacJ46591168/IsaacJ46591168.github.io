  • Personal Space

    Developed with: L Stands for Winner

    Made for the MQ RE-ENTRY Game Jam

    Play it here!

    A 3D Top-Down Game combining situational awareness and tight movement.

    Fly around in the depths of space while avoiding the spaceships trying to "re-enter" your atmosphere and avoid "re-entering" all the other planets while you're at it.

  • My Responsibilities

    3D Artist

    In this Game Jam, I was responsible for the creation and texturing of the spaceship and the asteroid models.

  • My Contributions

    Spaceships and Asteroids

    Since the models for this Game Jam were objects required to be avoided, not much detailling was given to them as the player wouldn't really be paying much more attention to them other than registering that they needed to be avoided, so the modelling and design was pretty basic. I did, however, take much inspiration from the Space Shuttle for the spaceship's design.
