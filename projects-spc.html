  • Personal Space

    Developed with: L Stands for Winner

    Made for the MQ RE-ENTRY Game Jam

    Play it here!

    A 3D Top-Down Game combining situational awareness and tight movement.

    Fly around in the depths of space while avoiding the spaceships trying to "re-enter" your atmosphere and avoid "re-entering" all the other planets while you're at it.

    In this Game Jam, I was responsible for the creation and texturing of the spaceship and the asteroid models.
